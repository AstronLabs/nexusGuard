Frequently Asked Questions

How is the pool risk calculated? expand_more
Risk is calculated via an algorithmic bonding curve that factors in historical claims, current liquidity, and the actuarial weight of the specific coverage type being requested.
Who governs the Nexus Guard protocol? expand_more
The protocol is governed by NXG token holders. All major upgrades, fee adjustments, and claim disputes are settled through our transparent on-chain voting system.
Are my funds safe in the liquidity pools? expand_more
Liquidity is locked in non-custodial smart contracts audited by OpenZeppelin and Trail of Bits. Our Capital Adequacy Ratio is maintained above 150% at all times.
